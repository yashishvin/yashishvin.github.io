Collabwatch

Collabwatch
One of the biggest problems with online learning is that there is not much human interaction involved, which leads students to feel disconnected, isolated and like they are not part of a community. This results in them not watching lectures and also lowers grades and productivity. CollabWatch will help solve this problem by mimicking interactions you would have in an in-person class. CollabWatch does this by having a space where you can watch both live and video lectures with friends or with strangers. Furthermore, in many classes teachers ask students to discuss or solve a particular problem with other students sitting near them. These conversations are stimulating, fun, and help create bonds between students inside the classroom. However, this is, unfortunately, not a possibility in online classes. But with CollabWatch this can become a reality. CollabWatch is a website that allows you to watch class lectures with your friends or with randomized strangers.
